Huangcoin

What is Huangcoin?

Huangcoin (HNC) is another peer-to-peer digital currency, but HNC is slightly different.


How is Huangcoin different?

Instead of being designed for the open market of the world, HNC was designed for use in the HTwins Central Discord server, and other HTwins services


Why use Huangcoin?

The prime reason someone would want to use HNC would be to perform transactions within HTwins communities. If you wished to use it for another purpose you could, but why?
